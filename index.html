Our Story

From Oyingbo
to Peckham

NAJA

Born from a Lagos mum's kitchen magic and a London street-food hustle. NAJA Kitchen blends unapologetic Nigerian flavours with the energy of UK's food renaissance.

Every dish is a love letter — smoky party jollof, fire-pepper suya, butter-soft puff puff, and Chapman cocktails that hit different.
